Why LeetKeys?

I built this site using pure JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. My goal was to make it as user-friendly and enjoyable as possible. Here are some of the key features:

  • Drag and Drop Avatar Upload: Easily upload your own avatar to add a personal touch to your profile.
  • Local Storage: Your scores, login details, and preferences are saved in your browser, so your data stays with you between sessions.
  • Real-Time Feedback and Sound Effects: The game gives instant visual feedback and plays sounds when you type correctly or make mistakes.
  • Smooth Animations: Enjoy subtle animations like fading, shaking, and sliding effects that make the experience lively and dynamic.
  • Responsive Design: The site looks great on all devices, ensuring a smooth experience whether you're on a computer or mobile.

Every part of LeetKeys is crafted to make testing your typing speed a delightful experience. Enjoy the game, and happy typing!
